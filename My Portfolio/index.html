About US

We are a producer and exporter that produces coconut and its derivatives from Indonesian nature. Our company is located in a country that has 17,000 islands with a vast coastline, high quality coconut raw materials are available in abundance. S.O.S Berkah Jaya is very concerned about the production process starting from the selection of quality materials, strict supervision during production and packaging so that the products we produce are of high quality. S.O.S Berkah Jaya has served customers from various countries in Europe, Asia and the Middle East. Our top priority is to meet customer satisfaction and expectations. Some of the products we supply include coconut charcoal, coconut charcoal briquettes shisha or BBQ, and other coconut derivative products. We are ready to work together for the long term corporation to provide our best products. Customer satisfaction is our priority

Product

Coconut shell charcoal is made from 100% sustained organic materials. Great for any purpose, especially food cooking and BBQ. This product have high calories with longer burning time and less ash

Coconut Briquette Charcoal is briquette charcoal with coconut shell as the raw material. One of the main functions of Coconut Briquette Charcoal is for shisha or hookah burner. There are many benefits to using coconut charcoal compare to other types of charcoal. Due to these tremendous benefits compared to other types of charcoal, the demand for coconut briquette charcoal is kept increasing over the years.

Coconut Charcoal Briquettes are manufactured entirely of pure Coconut Shell Charcoal. Coconut shell charcoal is a valuable product derived from coconut. Shell charcoal is used in a variety of applications, including home and industrial fuel, as well as barbeque and shisha hookah. Charcoal may also be produced into briquettes in different forms (square/cube, fingers, and hexagonal) in some countries.

Sawdust Charcoal Briquettes are briquettes made by briquettes of sawdust and other materials and then carbonizing. It has a high calorific value and carbon content.
